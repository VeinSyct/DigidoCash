FAQs

What is Digido ECash?

Digido ECash is an offline cash lending service designed to provide fast and accessible financial assistance. Clients receive cash-based loan credits for personal and emergency needs. Once the system is loaded, all loan records and balances are managed offline and stored locally on your device for privacy and control.

How does Digido ECash work?

After the system loads, Digido ECash operates entirely offline. Clients may request a cash loan, review the terms, and receive funds through authorized offline channels. Loan balances and repayment records are updated locally on your device. You may continue using the service until your loan is fully settled.

What are ECash loan credits?

ECash loan credits represent cash value issued to clients as part of a lending agreement. These credits are recorded locally on your device and reflect your outstanding loan balance. Initial credits may be issued based on eligibility and approval, subject to Digido ECash lending terms.

Where is my loan information stored?

All loan records, balances, and repayment information are stored locally on your device. Digido ECash does not retain remote copies of offline data. Clients are responsible for safeguarding their devices and maintaining access to their loan records.

What happens if I lose my loan records?

If your device is lost, erased, or damaged, locally stored loan data may be permanently lost. Digido ECash is not responsible for data loss caused by device issues. Clients are advised to keep personal documentation or receipts related to loan transactions.

How do I repay my loan?

Loan repayments are made through authorized offline payment points. Upon payment, your ECash loan balance is updated locally on your device. Proof of payment may be required to confirm and record successful repayment.

Is Digido ECash responsible for lost data?

No. All loan data is stored locally on the client’s device. Any loss resulting from deletion, device failure, or user error is the client’s responsibility. Digido ECash does not replace or recover lost offline records.

Are there fees or charges?

Loans may include applicable service fees or interest as outlined in the lending agreement. All charges are disclosed before loan approval. Clients are encouraged to review terms carefully prior to accepting any loan.

Is loan approval guaranteed?

Loan approval is not guaranteed and depends on eligibility criteria and internal assessment. Digido ECash encourages responsible borrowing and careful financial planning.

Can I use Digido ECash on mobile?

Yes. Once loaded, Digido ECash can be accessed offline on mobile devices. Loan records are stored locally. Clearing browser data or losing your device may result in permanent data loss, so clients should secure their devices properly.
